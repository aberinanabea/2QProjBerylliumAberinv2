Study Planning

Planning is one of the keys on how you can manage and do your work ahead your time.

Ways on how you can plan your day:

  1. Set a goal. You should know what you want in order to attain something. You may be confused at first but it's alright. The important part is that you can get back on track and work on yourself. Without goals, you are ging to have no direction, no ambition to be successful, no drive to stay in school, and trouble finding a career that will provide for you.

  2. Create a study plan. For a study plan, it will depend on you capability. Others can work at morning while some can't. So, they resort on studying late at night. If you think that your mind works at its fullest at night, you may study at that hour and have your sleep in the morning. Although this kind of routine can make you sick sometimes.

  3. Remove distractions. Social media became a part of this kinds of distractions especially to students. A new study found out that social media distraction in the classroom interferes with visual, but not auditory, learning in college students. As social media is becoming so much more popular, students all over the country and in various grades spend a large amount of their time communicating online. Social media is indeed distracting for many. In such circumstances, you may delete the apps that are not useful in your online learning.

  4. Take note your classes. Many students encounter instances wherein they are forget that they have classes. Likewise, you should be wary of your schedule.
